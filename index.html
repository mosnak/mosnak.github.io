How Does Solar Work?

Once your Solar System is installed, it will be producing Power during the day. Now, you will use your own system’s Power instead of the Grid Power. Previously, you were buying that day-time power from the grid. Now you’re not. That $$$ you’re saving by not buying electricity from the grid anymore, you use it to pay off your Solar System. As a result, within 2 to 3 years your system is completely paid off and you will be putting all that MONEY back into your savings account!!
